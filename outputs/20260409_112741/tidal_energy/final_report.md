**Tidal Energy Intelligence Brief**
=====================================

**Topic Overview**
-----------------

Tidal energy, a form of renewable energy that harnesses the power of ocean tides, is gaining momentum as a promising solution to address climate change and energy security challenges.

**Technical Classification/Background**
--------------------------------------

Tidal energy is a type of ocean energy that utilizes the predictable and reliable power generated by ocean tides. It offers a unique opportunity to provide baseload power, particularly in coastal regions.
